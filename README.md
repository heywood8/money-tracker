Penny is a personal finance tracking app for Android, built with React Native and Expo. It helps you stay on top of your money by managing accounts, logging transactions, organising categories, and visualising your spending over time.

## Features

- **Accounts** - Create and manage multiple financial accounts with custom currencies and running balances
- **Operations** - Log income and expense transactions linked to your accounts and categories
- **Categories** - Organise transactions with custom categories and icons
- **Graphs** - Visualise spending patterns and financial trends over time
- **Budgets** - Set and track spending limits per category

## Internationalisation

Penny supports 7 languages:

- English
- Italian
- Russian
- Spanish
- French
- Chinese
- German

## Themes

Choose between light, dark, or system-default theme from the settings.

## Platform

Android only.

## Privacy

Penny stores all data locally on your device. No data is sent to any server.
